About

Do you want to know about the team behind Origami Friends? Well, you’ve come to the right place! While we’re a small team, we have big hearts, and we hope this website and this game helps to bring a little joy and happiness to you all. And something to look forward to in the mail!

Our producer and writer Rachel E. Mailhot is leading the team over the summer, keeping everyone on track and to task as we tackle the remote aspects of this particular summer. With a writing concentration and experience working as a Production Assistant for Burden of Command (a professional in-development project with over 2.5k followers on Twitter), Rachel hopes to forge a team and company to push forward through this program, and (hopefully) beyond.
